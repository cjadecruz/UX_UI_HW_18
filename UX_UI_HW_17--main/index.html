About

Ciera Dela Cruz is a Filipino illustrator and UX/UI designer based in the United States. In matters of work, Ciera specializes in character design and UI design.

In non-work related matters, Ciera specializes in reading locked room mysteries and pretending to be able to solve locked room mysteries.

Previously, Ciera was known as an English major at Texas A&M University.
